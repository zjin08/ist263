Don't Define by Labels

In this video, we share our project "Don't Define by Labels," where we aimed to raise awareness about the harmful labels and stereotypes that are often placed on women. Through a display board featuring negative labels and high expectations, we encouraged people to reflect on the impact of these labels and challenge harmful societal norms. We also created four posters providing more information about our project and goals.

Through this video, you can witness the reactions of people as they engage with our project and participate in tearing off negative labels and replacing them with positive, empowering ones. You will see firsthand how we were able to promote body positivity, self-love, and empower women to embrace their unique identities.

By sharing this video, we hope to inspire others to challenge harmful societal norms and promote positive messages about self-acceptance and empowerment.
